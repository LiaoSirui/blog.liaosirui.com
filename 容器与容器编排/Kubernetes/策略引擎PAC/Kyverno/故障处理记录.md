MutatingWebhookConfiguration 和 ValidatingWebhookConfiguration 不会被考虑/发送到 webhook 的准入审查。

```yaml
apiVersion: admissionregistration.k8s.io/v1
kind: MutatingWebhookConfiguration
webhooks:
- name: webhookconfigurationtest
  failurePolicy: Ignore
  rules:
    - apiGroups:
        - "*"
      apiVersions:
        - "*"
      operations:
        - "*"
      resources:
        - mutatingwebhookconfigurations
        - validatingwebhookconfigurations
```

无法使用 kyverno 修改 MutatingWebhookConfiguration
